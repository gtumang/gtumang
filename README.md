### 🧠 About me

I'm an electrical engineering master's student at the **University of São Paulo (USP)**, where my interests sit at the intersection of **control systems, embedded electronics, and machine learning**. I like problems that touch hardware *and* software — especially those that encompass high-level intelligence and low-level embedded systems development.

---

### 🔭 Currently working on

- 🛰️ **Stewart-platform solar tracker** using **Extremum Seeking Control** with sinusoidal perturbation across 6 DOF
- ⚙️ **Control of inverted pendulum with reaction wheel** educational plant with real-time visualization and configuration
- 🤖 **Lane segmentation for automotive systems** using camera-based approach with deep neural networks and ROS2
- 🎓 Graduate ML coursework (**PSI5790**) at USP
- 🎓 Graduate Discrete-time Control Systems coursework (**PTC5611**) at USP

### 🚀 Featured projects

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

#### 🌞 Stewart Platform Solar Tracker
6-DOF parallel manipulator that tracks the sun using **Extremum Seeking Control** — no model of the sun's position required, just a light-intensity feedback loop with sinusoidal perturbation. Includes ESP32 firmware, custom power telemetry, and a hexagonal mechanical design.

Video demo: https://youtu.be/0Z71QivCWWU

`ESP-IDF` · `C` · `Control Theory` · `DSP`

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

#### 🎯 Reaction-Wheel Inverted Pendulum
Full-stack mechatronics build: **ESP32** firmware running a 50 Hz **LQR** state-space controller, paired with **MATLAB/Simulink** modeling, **Python** real-time telemetry over serial, and a custom **KiCad** PCB. Firmware, schematics, and Gerbers all in the repo.

`ESP32` · `C++` · `MATLAB` · `LQR` · `KiCad`
